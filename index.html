HERE YOU HAVE THE DIFERENTS TYPES OF CYCLING

In cycling, there are different modalities, for each type of person according to your tastes you can buy any of the following types of bicycles and even practice various types of cycling according to your preferences.

Road Cycling

Road cycling is the main type of cycling, the classic bikes were road and the great races that have the greatest prestige, such as the Tour de France, the Giro d'Italia or La Vuelta a Espa√±a, are road cycling; that is why it is so important to mention road cycling and its types.

Fixie Urban:

If you want to move around the city with agility, you should ride a fixie bike, with a single-speed fixed gear you can move with great skill, narrow handlebars to avoid crashing with obstacles, and if you prefer to use brakes or stop skidding around the city to have the best style while riding.

Track:

If you want to feel the speed without limits, track cycling is for you. A velodrome with a camber in the curves and a bike very similar to the fixie but much more aerodynamic and light will put you to the test and you will be able to break the speed limits in track cycling.

Time Trial:

For time trial events and long distances like those covered in triathlon, this is the kind of bike you should buy if you want to set great personal records and do many kilometers in the best possible time.

Mountain Biking

MountainBike (MTB) was born between the 60s and 70s in the United States, at a time when all cyclists were on the road, the MTB was created to generate a change among cyclists, a More Aggressive type of cycling and they began to try new things like jumps, and tricks that come from BMX, but now on roads and trails in mountains.

Do you like adrenaline and cycling? Ok,well. The mountain bike is for you. Here are the types of mountain biking\ that exist in the world.

Slopestyle:

Slopestyle is one of the most extreme forms of Mtb, it consists of doing tricks, for that you have to practice on tracks with large ramps, although you could go down on these bikes, its main objective is to do tricks.

Here we can see a slopestyle bike, it has no point of comparison with a road bike.

Cross Country XC:

You could already realize that the clothing in XC is similar to that of road cycling, because in XC although there is a lot of descent and uphill, as well as in road cycling, but with roots, mud, dirt, and the circumstances that present the terrain.

Downhill:

Downhill consists of descending, that is your goal. If you are a downhill rider you will wear a lot of protection and a helmet similar to that of a motorcycle.

Downhill is one of the most extreme sports, also it is so cool, if you are going downhill you must have nerves of steel.

Enduro:

Enduro is the midpoint between downhill and XC but with much more tendency to go down than XC, being able to go down very fast and do the odd trick is possible if you do enduro, the best of all is that if there is a climb you will not it will be a problem as if you were on a downhill cycle. The truth is that it is a very good option that could provide a lot of fun on the trails.
